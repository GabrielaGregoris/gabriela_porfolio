Casa Di Famiglia Restaurant Website

Designed a complete, family-friendly responsive website for Casa Di Famiglia, a fictional Italian restaurant offering authentic cuisine and interactive cooking classes. The project focused on creating a warm, inviting digital presence that seamlessly functions across desktop, tablet, and mobile breakpoints. Key deliverables included a clean, accessible interface, intuitive booking flows for reservations and classes, and a visual style that evokes a sense of home and tradition.Tools Used: Figma
